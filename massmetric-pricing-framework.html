Why Seat-Based and Subscription
The Strategic Rationale
01
Predictable Revenue
Usage-based models created ARR volatility during slow quarters. A seat-based subscription tied contract value to team size, a more stable growth signal, creating month-over-month revenue predictability.
02
Natural Expansion Path
As customers hired, they needed more seats. This created organic upsell moments without requiring sales intervention, embedding expansion directly into the customer's growth motion.
03
ACV Alignment
Per-seat pricing aligned cost with value received. Larger teams using more of the platform paid proportionally more, increasing ACV without changing the perceived price-per-user for buyers.
Pricing Architecture
Three-Tier Subscription Model
Model Decision
Annual subscription base with per-seat add-ons above each tier's included seat count. Monthly billing available at a 15% premium. Enterprise contracts structured as custom annual agreements with volume seat blocks.
