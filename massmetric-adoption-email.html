Feature Adoption Email

A lifecycle email designed to activate dormant users on MassCompete (MassMetric's competitive intelligence feature), contributing to a 22% lift in platform feature utilization.

Email Type
Lifecycle · Feature Activation
Trigger
Day 14, MassCompete not yet activated
Audience
Growth and Enterprise accounts with active seats, zero MassCompete events
Goal
Drive first competitive report and reduce churn risk
Strategy
Urgency without pressure. "Your competitors just moved" creates relevance-based urgency, not artificial scarcity. The hook is about their world, not our feature.
Copywriting
Signal preview = value preview. Showing 3 real-looking competitive signals before the click teaches users what the feature does and removes activation anxiety.
UX
Three steps removes friction. Users who don't activate often fear complexity. Showing it takes 3 steps under 5 minutes eliminates the "I'll do it later" deferral.

Trigger Logic
Why Day 14?
CRM data showed the highest activation drop-off at days 10 through 18. Users who hadn't touched MassCompete by day 14 had a 3x higher likelihood of never activating. Day 14 was the optimal intervention point — late enough for onboarding dust to settle, early enough to save the account.
Sequencing
Three-Email Escalation
Email 1 (Day 14): Value-first. Shows signals, low pressure. Email 2 (Day 21): Social proof. "Here's what teams like yours found." Email 3 (Day 30): Urgency plus CS offer. "Book 20 min with your CSM, we'll set it up for you." Each escalation step reduced required user effort.
Impact
Why This Mattered
MassCompete was included in the Growth plan but had the lowest activation rate of all 7 MassMetric products. Low activation equals perceived low value equals churn risk at renewal. This email series was the primary driver of the 22% feature utilization lift, directly strengthening account retention.
