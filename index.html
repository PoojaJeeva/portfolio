Technical Product Marketing

Translating complex products into market moments.

Right now I am a Technical Product Marketing Manager at Laser Photonics, where I own the messaging, positioning, and sales enablement for AI-powered laser platforms. I sit in C-suite meetings, turn what I hear into pitch decks and battle cards, and help technical products reach the enterprise and federal buyers who need them. I also lead investor relations and have placed campaigns on CNBC and Bloomberg.

Before this I rebuilt AI platform positioning at MassMetric using customer interviews and win/loss data, shortening enterprise sales cycles 20% and driving a 22% feature adoption lift. Across both roles I have been building AI tooling to cut the time from research to collateral from days to minutes. If the product is complex and the buyer is skeptical, that is exactly where I want to be.
